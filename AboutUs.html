Why sign language ?

Sign language is a vital form of communication for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community. It promotes equal access to information, fosters inclusion, and breaks down communication barriers between hearing and non-hearing individuals. By using multimedia content, learning sign language becomes more engaging, accessible, and effective for people of all ages and abilities.

Vision

To become the main digital platform in empowering universal communication through interactive and multimedia sign language learning.

Mission

Providing an interactive and easily accessible sign language learning platform through multimedia content to support inclusive communication and community awareness.
